Day-by-Day Itinerary
PHASE 1: CEBU Days 2–5 · Whale Sharks · Canyoneering · Island Hopping
DAY 1 Fri 18 Dec – London Departure▼

Evening – Check in at Emirates or Qatar Airways Business/Economy lounge at LHR T3/T4. Overnight departure via Dubai or Doha.

Overnight: In flight

DAY 2 Sat 19 Dec – Arrival in Cebu▼

Arrive Mactan-Cebu International Airport, late afternoon. Private transfer to beachfront resort on Mactan Island (30–45 min). Light dinner and rest.

Hotels: Shangri-La Mactan (£180–320/night) · Crimson Resort (£150–250/night) · Plantation Bay (£120–200/night)

Overnight: Cebu

DAY 3 Sun 20 Dec – Whale Sharks & Waterfalls▼

05:00 – Depart for Oslob (3–3.5h south). Swim with whale sharks in regulated morning sessions.

09:00 – Whale shark interaction in Oslob shallows.

12:00 – Lunch at local seafood restaurant.

13:30 – Tumalog Falls – stunning curtain waterfall, 15 min from Oslob.

Evening – Larsian BBQ night market in Cebu City.

Overnight: Cebu

DAY 4 Mon 21 Dec – Canyoneering at Kawasan Falls▼

07:00 – Depart for Kawasan Falls (3–4h). Half-day canyoneering: cliff jumping, canyon scrambles, turquoise pools.

Evening – Cebu Heritage Trail: Magellan's Cross, Basilica del Santo Niño, Fort San Pedro.

Overnight: Cebu

DAY 5 Tue 22 Dec – Island Hopping▼

08:00 – Full-day island hopping: Hilutungan Marine Sanctuary · Nalusuan Island · Pandanon Island. Fresh seafood lunch on the boat.

Evening – Farewell Cebu dinner: pochero and kinilaw.

Overnight: Cebu

PHASE 2: CORON Days 6–9 · Kayangan Lake · WWII Wrecks · Christmas Eve
DAY 6 Wed 23 Dec – Fly to Coron▼

Morning – Flight Cebu → Coron/Busuanga (~1h 25min). Check in.

14:00 – Maquinit Hot Springs – rare saltwater thermal pools (~40°C) surrounded by mangroves.

17:00 – Sunset from Mount Tapyas (724 steps, panoramic 360° view).

Hotels: Two Seasons Coron (£200–400/night) · Club Paradise Palawan (£180–350/night) · Coron Soleil Garden (£70–120/night)

Overnight: Coron

DAY 7 Thu 24 Dec – Christmas Eve Island Hopping▼

07:30 – Ultimate Coron Tour by bangka: Kayangan Lake · Twin Lagoon · Barracuda Lake · Coral Garden · CYC Beach.

Evening – Special Christmas Eve dinner with live music and Noche Buena feasting.

Overnight: Coron

DAY 8 Fri 25 Dec – Christmas Day · WWII Wreck Diving▼

Morning – Sunrise Mass or relaxation at resort.

Afternoon – Optional: Diving at Coron's 12 sunken Japanese WWII warships – among the best wreck diving in the world.

Overnight: Coron

DAY 9 Sat 26 Dec – Remote Islands▼

07:00 – Full-day excursion: Malcapuya Island · Banana Island · Bulog Dos sandbar. Pristine, very few visitors. Beach picnic lunch.

Overnight: Coron

PHASE 3: EL NIDO & NEW YEAR Days 10–16 · Lagoons · Hidden Beaches · NYE Under Stars
DAY 10 Sun 27 Dec – Coron → El Nido▼

07:00 – Fast ferry (3.5–5h, scenic) or AirSWIFT flight (40min) to El Nido. Check in. Sunset at Corong-Corong Beach.

Hotels: Pangulasian Island Resort (£350–600/night) · Miniloc Island Resort (£250–450/night) · Frangipani El Nido (£80–150/night)

Overnight: El Nido

DAY 11 Mon 28 Dec – Tour A: Big & Small Lagoon▼

El Nido's iconic Tour A by bangka: Big Lagoon (kayak through limestone cliffs) · Small Lagoon (hidden cave entrance) · Secret Lagoon · Shimizu Island snorkelling.

Overnight: El Nido

DAY 12 Tue 29 Dec – Tour C: Hidden Beaches▼

Tour C: Matinloc Shrine (dramatic cliff viewpoint) · Secret Beach (swim through cliff gap) · Hidden Beach · Helicopter Island snorkelling.

Overnight: El Nido

DAY 13 Wed 30 Dec – Nacpan Beach▼

08:00 – Drive to Nacpan Beach (45min) – 4km of golden sand, consistently one of the world's best beaches. Afternoon kayaking in Bacuit Bay.

Overnight: El Nido

DAY 14 Thu 31 Dec – New Year's Eve▼

18:00 – NYE celebrations: fireworks, beach parties, fire dancers, midnight countdown on the sand. One of the most spectacular New Year's Eve spots in the world.

🎆 El Nido is magical at New Year – barefoot, warm air, and the stars above Bacuit Bay.

Overnight: El Nido

DAY 15 Fri 1 Jan – Recovery Day▼

New Year's Day rest. Pool, beach, or optional Tour B (caves, Snake Island sandbar). Final El Nido dinner.

Overnight: El Nido

DAY 16 Sat 2 Jan – El Nido → Manila → London▼

Transfer to Lio Airport. Fly El Nido → Manila (AirSWIFT ~1h 15min). Connect to Qatar Airways or Emirates back to London. Arrive London 3 January morning.

Overnight: In flight

Day-by-Day Itinerary
PHASE 1: THE CORDILLERA MOUNTAINS Days 1–6 · Banaue UNESCO Terraces · Sagada Caves · Indigenous Culture
DAY 1 Fri 18 Dec – London → Manila▼

Evening – Depart LHR. Overnight via Dubai (Emirates) or Doha (Qatar). Arrive Manila late Sat.

Overnight: In flight

DAY 2 Sat 19 Dec – Manila → Night Bus to Banaue▼

Arrive Manila afternoon. Transfer to Cubao bus terminal. Board premium overnight sleeper bus to Banaue (~9–10h). Saves a hotel night!

💡 Grab dinner near Cubao terminal. Florida Bus and OhMyBus are the best operators.

Overnight: Sleeper bus

DAY 3 Sun 20 Dec – Banaue & Batad Rice Terraces▼

06:00 – Arrive Banaue. Jeepney to Batad Saddle, then hike down into Batad village. UNESCO World Heritage amphitheatre terraces – 2,000 years old. Tappiya Falls hike (30m cascade). Return to Banaue.

Hotels: Banaue Hotel (£20–50/night) · People's Lodge (£15–25/night) · Batad Homestay (£10–20/night – recommended!)

Overnight: Banaue / Batad

DAY 4 Mon 21 Dec – Journey to Sagada▼

07:00 – 3–4h mountain drive to Sagada through pine forests and Ifugao villages. Afternoon: Hanging Coffins of Echo Valley (guided walk). Sunset at Kiltepan Peak.

Hotels: Misty Lodge & Cafe (£25–45/night) · George Guesthouse (£15–25/night)

Overnight: Sagada

DAY 5 Tue 22 Dec – Sumaguing Cave Adventure▼

09:00 – Sumaguing Cave Connection – wade underground rivers, climb limestone cliffs, squeeze through passages. Mandatory guide (register at Tourism Office). You will get completely wet!

Afternoon – Optional: Bomod-Ok Falls hike, or rest with hot tea and a book.

Overnight: Sagada

DAY 6 Wed 23 Dec – Long Road to Manila▼

05:00 – Pre-dawn departure. Bus via Baguio (~10–12h). Lunch stop in Baguio (strawberry market, ube jam). Arrive Manila evening. Hot shower, rest, and a proper meal.

Hotels in Manila: The Peninsula Makati (£120–250/night) · Seda BGC (£80–140/night) · Raffles Makati (£200–400/night)

Overnight: Manila

PHASE 2: SUGAR HERITAGE & FOOD TRAIL Days 7–10 · Iloilo · Guimaras · Bacolod · Western Visayas Cuisine
DAY 7 Thu 24 Dec – Fly to Iloilo – Christmas Eve▼

Morning flight Manila → Iloilo (~1h). La Paz Batchoy lunch (original Deco's or Ted's). Heritage church tour: Miag-ao Church (UNESCO) · Molo Church.

Evening – Noche Buena Christmas Eve dinner: pancit molo, lechon de leche, fresh seafood.

Hotels: Richmonde Hotel Iloilo (£60–100/night) · Seda Atria (£55–90/night)

Overnight: Iloilo

DAY 8 Fri 25 Dec – Guimaras Island▼

08:00 – 15-min pumpboat to Guimaras. Tricycle tour of the island. World's sweetest Carabao mangoes straight from the tree. Mango pizza (!). Alubihod Beach for swimming.

Evening – Tatoy's Restaurant for chargrilled native chicken.

Overnight: Iloilo

DAY 9 Sat 26 Dec – Fast Ferry to Bacolod▼

Ferry Iloilo → Bacolod (1.5h). Lunch: Bacolod Chicken Inasal at Manokan Country – the unmissable original. Afternoon: The Ruins at Talisay (arrive 16:30 for golden hour).

Hotels: The Henry Hotel (£80–130/night) · L'Fisher Hotel (£50–80/night)

Overnight: Bacolod

DAY 10 Sun 27 Dec – Negros Road Trip → Dumaguete▼

Ceres bus / hired car south along Negros coast to Dumaguete (5–6h). Sugar cane, volcanoes, fishing villages. Evening: Rizal Boulevard promenade, barbecue stalls.

Hotels: The Thinker's Lodge (£35–60/night) · South Sea Resort (£50–85/night)

Overnight: Dumaguete

PHASE 3: SIQUIJOR – MYSTIC ISLAND & NYE Days 11–17 · Sea Turtles · Rope Swings · Healers · New Year Barefoot
DAY 11 Mon 28 Dec – Apo Island → Siquijor▼

07:00 – Boat to Apo Island (40min). Snorkel with wild green sea turtles – one of the best encounters in Southeast Asia. Return to Dumaguete, then ferry to Siquijor (1h).

Resorts: Coco Grove Beach Resort (£80–150/night) · Charisma Beach Resort (£50–90/night) · Islander's Paradise (£30–55/night)

Overnight: Siquijor

DAY 12 Tue 29 Dec – Island Scooter Loop▼

Rent scooters (£8–12/day). Circumferential road loop: Balete Tree & fish spa · Cambugahay Falls (rope swing into turquoise water!) · Lazi Convent (oldest in Asia) · Paliton Beach at sunset.

Overnight: Siquijor

DAY 13 Wed 30 Dec – Beach Day & Folk Healers▼

Salagdoong Beach (cliff jumping). Optional: visit a mananambal folk healer in the mountains – a unique experience found nowhere else in the Philippines. Lugnason Falls. Hilot massage at sunset.

Overnight: Siquijor

DAY 14 Thu 31 Dec – New Year's Eve▼

Relaxed morning. Afternoon hilot massage. Evening: San Juan beach bars come alive – bonfires, fire dancers, live music, midnight fireworks over the sea. Intimate, warm, magical.

🎆 Siquijor's NYE is intimate and magical vs mainstream. Barefoot, stars, warm ocean.

Overnight: Siquijor

DAY 15 Fri 1 Jan – Recovery Day▼

New Year's Day. Fresh coconuts, pool, ocean. Gentle boat trip if energy permits. Final dinner in Siquijor: whole grilled fish and cold San Miguel.

Overnight: Siquijor

DAY 16 Sat 2 Jan – Siquijor → Dumaguete → Manila▼

Morning ferry back to Dumaguete. Flight Dumaguete → Manila (1h 15min). Afternoon rest at airport hotel before night departure to London.

Overnight: In flight

DAY 17 Sun 3 Jan – Arrive London▼

Arrive LHR morning/early afternoon via Dubai or Doha. Journey complete.

Head-to-Head: What Each Plan Offers
🏖 Beach & Ocean Quality
Plan 1

World-class beaches: Nacpan, Malcapuya, CYC Beach. El Nido lagoons are arguably the best in the Philippines.

Plan 2

Quieter, less-visited beaches: Alubihod (Guimaras), Salagdoong (Siquijor), Paliton Beach. Less photogenic, more authentic.

🍽 Food Experience
Plan 1

Good seafood in Cebu and El Nido. Larsian BBQ, kinilaw. Beach grills.

Plan 2

Outstanding. La Paz Batchoy, Bacolod Chicken Inasal, Guimaras mangoes. The Visayas is the culinary capital of the Philippines.

🏛 Culture & Heritage
Plan 1

Heritage Trail in Cebu City. Mostly a nature/activity-based trip.

Plan 2

Rich: 2,000-year-old rice terraces, UNESCO churches, folk healers, ancestral houses, Spanish colonial towns throughout.

🧗 Adventure Level
Plan 1

Canyoneering at Kawasan, WWII wreck diving, snorkelling. Moderate-high.

Plan 2

Higher: Batad terrace hike, Sumaguing Cave (demanding), cliff jumping in Siquijor, plus long mountain transit days.

😌 Relaxation Factor
Plan 1

High – resort-based stays, beach days built in. El Nido is laid-back.

Plan 2

Mixed – Cordillera phase is tough. Siquijor (Days 11–17) is deeply restful. Best rest of the two trips in final phase.

🚌 Travel Fatigue
Plan 1

Moderate. Flights between islands. El Nido connection can be long.

Plan 2

Higher. 10–12h bus days, overnight sleeper, multiple ferries. Rewarding but tiring.

🎆 New Year's Eve
Plan 1

El Nido: beach party, fireworks over Bacuit Bay. Festive and lively.

Plan 2

Siquijor: intimate, barefoot, bonfires, fire dancers. More magical, less crowded.

📸 Instagram Factor
Plan 1

Very high. El Nido lagoons are globally recognised. High visual impact throughout.

Plan 2

High but different. Batad terraces are breathtaking. Hanging Coffins, The Ruins, Cambugahay Falls are striking but less mainstream.
